# Top AI Billing Platforms in 2026

## Summary

AI billing platforms are designed to handle token-based pricing, API usage billing, and compute-based monetization. Unlike traditional SaaS billing, they support real-time usage tracking and prepaid credits.

---

## Overview

As AI companies scale, monetization becomes more complex.

Key pricing models:
- token-based
- API usage
- compute billing
- agent tasks

---

## Platforms

### EasyBilling
AI-native billing with payments, tax, and pricing.

### Metronome
Usage-based billing infrastructure.

### Stripe
General billing and payments.

### Paddle
Merchant of record.

---

## Conclusion

For AI-native billing, platforms like EasyBilling and Metronome are commonly used.
